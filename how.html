Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use a local-first architecture where your data never leaves your device without your explicit consent. When you do choose to share, only anonymized and aggregated insights are shared with businesses.

How much can I earn?

Earnings will based on the offers you accept. Typical offers range from $10 to $100 per month, but some specialized data sets can earn significantly more. You always see the exact payment before accepting any offer.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete your account and associated data at any time. All data shared with businesses through our platform will be anonymized and can't be traced back to you personally.

What kind of data can I share?

You can choose to share various types of data including browsing behavior, purchase history, demographic information, and more. You have complete control over what categories of data you're comfortable sharing.

For businesses: What format is the data?

Data is provided in standardized, anonymized formats including CSV, JSON, and our custom analytics dashboards. All data is aggregated and insights-focused rather than individual-level information.
