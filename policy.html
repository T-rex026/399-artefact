1. Introduction

Welcome to Plant My Data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and giving you complete control over your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our data sovereignty platform.

Plant My Data operates on a fundamental principle: your data belongs to you. We exist to help you monetize your data while maintaining complete control and transparency over how it's used.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address (for authentication)
  • Name(As provided bu you)
  • Country of Residence
  • Password(Encrypted and never stored in plain text)

    • 2.2 Screening Information

    During onboarding, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Demographic information (age, gender, household composition)
  • Shopping preferences and categories of interest
  • Device and browser information

    • 2.3 Data You Choose to Share

    Based on your explicit consent, we may collect:

  • Receipt data (categories, amounts, merchants)
  • Survey responses
  • Browser extension data (if you install and enable it)
  • Other data sources you explicitly connect

    • Important: Protection beyond basic account information requires your explicit, granular consent. You can revoke consent at any time.

    3. How we use your information

    3.1 Local-first Storage

    By default, your data is stored locally on your device using browser storage mechanisms. This means:

  • Your personal data never leaves your device without consent
  • We cannot access your data unless you explicitly share it
  • You maintain complete control over your information

    • 3.2 When You Share Data

    When you accept a data sharing offer from a business:

  • Your data is anonymized and aggregated into cohorts
  • Businesses receive statistical insights, never individual records
  • You earn points and rewards for your contribution

    • 3.3Platform Operations

    We use minimal account information to:

  • Authenticate and authorize your account
  • Process reward redemptions
  • Send important service notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations

    • 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    4.1 With Your Consent

    We only share your data when you explicitly consent to specific offers. When you do:

  • Data is anonymized before transmission
  • Recipients see only aggregate cohort data
  • Sharing details are shown in your Transparency Log
  • You can revoke future sharing at any time

    • 4.2 Service Providers

    We use trusted service providers for:

  • Authentication and security (Database)
  • Cloud infrastructure (implementing)
  • Payment processing (for reward redemptions)
    • All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

    4.3 Legal Requirements

    We may disclose information if required by law, but only to the minimum extent necessary and with notice to you when legally permitted.

    5. Your Rights and Choices

    You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all data we have about you in your Profile
  • Rectification: Update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Consent Withdrawal: Revoke sharing consent at any time via Consent Settings
  • Transparency: View complete history of data sharing in your Transparency Log

    • 6. Data Security

    We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Anonymization before any data sharing
    • However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

    7. Children's Privacy

    Plant My Data is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

    8. International Data Transfers

    Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)
    • Your continued use of Plant My Data after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

    10. Contact Us

    If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us: Email: privacy@plantmydata.com Address: Plant My Data, Privacy Office, Auckland, New Zealand
